Press the Spacebar to start the game
Press the Spacebar to jump through the pipes
When the player eats the red triangle, gravity will invert and there will be 8 seconds of
invincibility.
Also when gravity is inverted the spacebar will be pressed to make the player "fall"
The game ends when the player crashes into the pipes or the floor or the ceiling and there is
a death particle effect.
Press Space to restart the game.
